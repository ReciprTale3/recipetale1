Turkish shakshuka for breakfast

⌛ CooK Time : 10min

Ingredients :

  1. A spoonful of olive oil

  2. A spoonful of olive oil

  3. Paprika (half a teaspoon)

  4. 2 eggs

  5. 1 finely chopped capsicum

  6. 1 tomato, finely chopped, 200 gr

  7. 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped

  8. 2 minced garlic cloves

  9. Chopped parsley to taste

lnstructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C

  2. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add onions and garlic and stir until wilted.

  3. Add red hot pepper, paprika and turkey pepper. Stir the mixture for a minute

  4. Add the tomatoes. Boil the mixture for 5 minutes

  5. Break the eggs over the mixture. Insert the pan into the oven for 5 minutes, until the eggs are cooked

  6. Remove the pan from the oven. Garnish with parsley and serve hot shakshuka. health!

(2)

French Toast

⌛ CooK Time : 20min

Ingredients :

  1. 6 large eggs

  2. 1 1/2 c. whole milk

  3. 1 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon

  4. 1 1/2 tsp. pure vanilla extract

  5. 8 1-inch-thick slices challah bread

  6. 4 tbsp. unsalted butter

  7. Yogurt, berries, and pure maple syrup or honey, for serving

Instructions :

  1. In large, shallow bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla.

  2. Working in batches, place 2 bread slices in egg mixture and let soak 2 minutes. Flip and soak 1 minute more (both sides of bread should be totally coated in mixture).

  3. Meanwhile, heat 1 tablespoon butter in large nonstick skillet on medium-low. Once melted, add soaked bread and cook until golden brown, 1 to 3 minutes per side; transfer to wire rack. While toast is cooking, soak next batch of challah slices.

  4. Repeat with remaining butter and bread. Serve topped with yogurt, berries, and syrup or honey if desired.
